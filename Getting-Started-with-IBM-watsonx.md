# Getting Started with IBM watsonx

## Introduction

**IBM watsonx** is IBM's enterprise-ready AI and data platform designed to help organizations build, deploy, govern, and scale Artificial Intelligence solutions responsibly.

The platform combines **Generative AI, Foundation Models, Machine Learning, Data Management, and AI Governance** into a unified ecosystem that enables organizations to accelerate innovation while maintaining trust, security, and compliance.

This guide provides a beginner-friendly introduction to getting started with IBM watsonx.

---

## What is IBM watsonx?

**IBM watsonx** is IBM's next-generation Enterprise AI platform consisting of three core components:

- **IBM watsonx.ai** – AI development studio
- **IBM watsonx.data** – Open and scalable data lakehouse
- **IBM watsonx.governance** – Responsible AI governance platform

Together, these components help organizations build and manage AI solutions at enterprise scale.
