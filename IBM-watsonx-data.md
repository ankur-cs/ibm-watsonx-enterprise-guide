**IBM watsonx.data**

**Introduction**

**IBM watsonx.data** is the data foundation component of the **IBM watsonx platform**. It is designed to help organizations manage, store, access, and analyze large volumes of Enterprise Data efficiently.

As Artificial Intelligence (AI) becomes increasingly dependent on high-quality data, **IBM watsonx.data provides a scalable and cost-effective solution that supports AI, Analytics, and Business Intelligence workloads**.

---

**What is IBM watsonx.data**?

IBM watsonx.data is an open and scalable Data Lakehouse platform that combines the flexibility of a Data Lake with the performance of a Data Warehouse.

**It enables organizations to**:

- Store structured and unstructured data
- Access Enterprise Data efficiently
- Support Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning workloads
- Improve analytics performance
- Reduce infrastructure costs
- Scale data operations across Hybrid Cloud environments

**IBM watsonx.data helps businesses unlock the full value of their data while maintaining flexibility and control**.

---

**Why IBM Developed watsonx.data**

Modern organizations generate massive amounts of data every day.

**Common challenges include**:

- Data silos
- High storage costs
- Complex data management
- Slow analytics performance
- Difficulty preparing data for AI systems

**IBM developed watsonx.data to address these challenges and create a modern data architecture optimized for Artificial Intelligence and analytics**.
