**IBM watsonx.governance**

**Introduction**

IBM watsonx.governance is the governance and responsible AI component of the IBM watsonx platform. It helps organizations manage, monitor, govern, and scale Artificial Intelligence systems while maintaining transparency, accountability, and trust.

As AI becomes increasingly integrated into business operations, organizations need tools that ensure AI systems are ethical, compliant, explainable, and secure. IBM watsonx.governance was designed to address these critical requirements.

---

**What is IBM watsonx.governance**?

IBM watsonx.governance is an enterprise-grade AI governance solution that enables organizations to manage the entire AI lifecycle.

**It helps organizations**:

- Govern AI models
- Monitor AI performance
- Ensure regulatory compliance
- Improve transparency
- Reduce operational risk
- Build trustworthy AI systems

The platform provides a centralized framework for managing AI responsibly at scale.

---

**Why AI Governance Matters**

As AI adoption grows, organizations face challenges such as:

- Bias in AI models

IBM watsonx.governance helps organizations address these challenges through responsible AI practices.
