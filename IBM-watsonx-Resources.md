**What is IBM watsonx**?

IBM watsonx is IBM's enterprise-ready AI and data platform that helps organizations build, deploy, manage, and govern AI solutions responsibly.

**The platform consists of**:

- IBM watsonx.ai
- IBM watsonx.data
- IBM watsonx.governance

Together, these components enable organizations to accelerate AI adoption while maintaining security, transparency, and trust.
